Cardiac Perfusionist

Job Description - Cardiac perfusionists are people who assist surgeons with open heart surgery. Cardiac Perfusionists operate the machine that pumps the patient’s blood while the heart is stopped for surgery.

  • Salary Info - Cardiac Perfusionists earn between about $70,000 - $90,000.
  • Job Availability - Since this is a very hard and competitive field, there are usually only a few openings in the United States.
  • Education Requirement - Cardiac Perfusionists require a bachelor’s degree and the completion of an accredited training program in clinical cardiac perfusion.


Source: Verywellhealth.com
